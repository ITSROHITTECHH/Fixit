1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

Fixit uses cookies to improve our platform and provide better services.

  • Remember your login session
  • Save user preferences
  • Improve website performance
  • Analyze traffic and user behavior

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic functionality.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the website.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings.
  • Analytics Cookies: Used to measure traffic and performance.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect certain features of the Fixit website.

5. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be set by third-party services such as analytics providers or embedded content providers.

6. Policy Updates

Fixit may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology or legal requirements.
